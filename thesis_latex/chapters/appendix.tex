\chapter{Monthly Carbon Intensity}
\label{app:carbon_monthly}

\Cref{tab:app_carbon_monthly} summarizes the carbon intensity of the German electricity grid for each calendar month of 2024, derived from 8{,}760 hourly observations obtained via the ElectricityMaps API. These values underpin the seasonal counterfactual analysis in \Cref{sec:results_carbon}. The winter months (October--January) consistently show higher mean intensity than the summer months, driven by reduced solar generation and higher gas and coal dispatch. November records the highest monthly mean; June records the single lowest hourly minimum.
